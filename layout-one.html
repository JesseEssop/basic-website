    Historic Places in CPT
    Iziko Musuem
    • The South African Museum was founded by Lord Charles Somerset in 1825.
    • Between 1907 and 1924, sixty-eight body casts of "pure Bushmen specimens" were taken.
    • Subsequent research on Bushmen was informed by the rise of physical anthropology.
    City Hall
    • The City Hall's carillon was installed as a World War I war memorial.
    • The building was designed as the result of a public competition.
    • The tower of the City Hall has a Turret Clock which strikes the hours and chimes the Westminster quarters.
    Castle Of Goodhope
    • Easily recognized by its mustardopular attraction.
    • Visitors can explore the supposedly haunted hallways in the evening.
