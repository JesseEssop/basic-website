My favorite city

The reason I chose Paris as my favourite city is because it is a place with a lot of history. Besides the Eiffel Tower I was always really fascinated by experiencing the infrastructure and the culture in Paris. I also feel a sense of tranquility, just existing in a moment of silence and looking at art, because for me it's almost like meditating. Trying french cuisines in the country it originated from, is also a big dream of mine. I truly believe that I will be able to go to Paris, and it will be because of my music.

Historic Places in CPT

Iziko Museum

  • The South African Museum was founded by Lord Charles Somerset in 1825.
  • Between 1907 and 1924, sixty-eight body casts of "pure Bushmen specimens" were taken.
  • Subsequent research on Bushmen was informed by the rise of physical anthropology.

City Hall

  • The City Hall's carillon was installed as a World War I war memorial.
  • The building was designed as the result of a public competition.
  • The tower of the City Hall has a Turret Clock which strikes the hours and chimes the Westminster quarters.

Castle Of Goodhope

  • Easily recognized by its mustardopular attraction.
  • Visitors can explore the supposedly haunted hallways in the evening.
  • Horseshoes hung up in several areas, they were put there for good luck.
