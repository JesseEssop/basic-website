    Bo-Kaap
    • Original residents were descendants of slaves brought in from Africa and Asia.
    • End of the holy month and many of the residents then paint their houses brightly coloured
    • Group Areas Act of 1950, declared the Bo Kaap a Muslims-only area.

    • The vibe here is always amazing.
    • Many free concerts that take place on Greenmarket Square.
    • It’s loud, colourful, and at times almost dizzying with activity.
